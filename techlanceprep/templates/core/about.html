About TechlancePrep

Your journey to mastering coding starts here

Our Mission

TechlancePrep was created with a simple mission: to make quality coding education accessible to everyone, completely free of cost. We believe that financial constraints should never stop someone from learning and growing in their career.

What We Offer

  • Curated coding questions from various difficulty levels
  • Multiple programming topics including DSA, Python, React, and Django
  • Progress tracking to monitor your improvement
  • Bookmark feature to save questions for later practice
  • Responsive design for learning on any device

Who This Is For

Whether you're a complete beginner just starting your coding journey, a college student preparing for placements, or a professional looking to brush up your skills, TechlancePrep has something for everyone.

Technology Stack

Built with Django, Tailwind CSS, and PostgreSQL. This project itself serves as a learning resource for those interested in full-stack web development with Python.
